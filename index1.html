Tayrona

Tayrona National Park -

Tayrona National Park is a must-see place for travelers who enjoy nature and exploring old ruins.
Located on the north Colombia coast, the park offers a variety of ecosystems, from swamps to
scrublands to cloud forests. For many travelers, the park’s biggest attraction is its beaches, set
in deep bays and shaded with coconut palms.
In fact, Tayrona beaches are among Colombia’s most beautiful. The park is also popular with
birdwatchers who come to see the Andean condor, a threatened species. The park takes its name
from the Tayronas, a native people, who left their mark on a number of archaeological sites,
including Pueblito Chairama. Tayrona is a good place to snorkel, hike and see wild animals,
including lizards and monkeys, in their native habitat.
